We Offer

We offer the full spectrum of services to help organizations work better.Yatra is a comprehensive software solution that revolutionizes the way hotels operate, streamline their operations, and enhance guest experiences. It serves as a centralized platform to manage and automate various hotel functions, including reservations, check-ins, housekeeping, billing, reporting, and more.

Reservations and Booking Management

Yatra allows guests to make online reservations and bookings through various channels such as the hotel's website, mobile app, or third-party booking platforms. It streamlines the process of managing room availability, rates, and guest preferences, helping to maximize occupancy and revenue.

Front Desk Operations

Yatra provides tools to manage front desk operations, including guest check-in and check-out, room assignment, and guest information management. It helps hotel staff handle guest inquiries and requests promptly, improving customer service.

Channel Management

Manages distribution and updates of room availability and rates across various online travel agencies (OTAs) and booking platforms to maximize visibility and bookings.

Guest Relationship Management

Manages guest profiles, preferences, and communication history for personalized service and targeted marketing.

Housekeeping and Maintenance Management

The system assists in tracking room cleaning schedules, maintenance tasks, and room status updates. It helps coordinate communication between housekeeping and maintenance staff to ensure rooms are well-maintained and available for guests in a timely manner.

Reporting and Analytics

Yatra gathers and analyzes data related to bookings, revenue, guest preferences, and other key performance indicators. This data-driven insight enables hotel managers to make informed decisions, identify areas for improvement, and implement strategies to enhance guest satisfaction and increase profitability.
